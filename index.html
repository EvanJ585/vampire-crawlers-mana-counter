Wild Cards

Wild cards have no mana number. They can be used at any point to start a combo or fill a missing step so the sequence can continue.

They have no numbered cost, so you do not need to chase a target count for them. Run as many as feels good.

Weapon Evolutions

This tab is a reminder for weapon evolutions: the first card plus its matching support card can combine into the stronger card shown after the equals sign.

The blue orb at the end is the mana cost of the evolved card, so you can check whether the upgraded card still fits your deck curve.
